What I learned: Introduction to Critical Infrastructure Protection (ICIP)

A starter lens on critical infrastructure protection and how OPSWAT frames learning in that space.

In short

Why sectors like energy and healthcare need different security thinking, how certification paths are organized, and how to navigate OPSWAT Academy as a learner.

The credential

OPSWAT Introduction to Critical Infrastructure Protection (ICIP). Verify on Credly.

What “critical infrastructure” means

These are systems where outages or breaches can affect public safety or national resilience—power grids, water treatment, hospitals, transport, and more. Security there mixes cyber controls with physical and operational reality.

What I took away

  • A clearer map of CIP concepts before diving into vendor-specific tools.
  • How OPSWAT Academy structures modules and assessments.
  • Context for secure transfer and hardening conversations when files cross trust boundaries.
